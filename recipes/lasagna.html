Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a baked Italian dish made with layers of pasta sheets, sauce, and other ingredients. The pasta is usually wide, flat, and often ruffled, and is made from a dough of flour and eggs. Lasagna can be made with a variety of fillings, such as ragù, bechamel, vegetables, and cheeses.

ingredients

  • Flour
  • eggs
  • ragù
  • bechamel
  • vegetables
  • cheese filling

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Prepare meat sauce
  3. Combine cheese mixture
  4. Layer and bake
